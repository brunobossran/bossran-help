Level Guide
This guide helps new players understand how to level, reborn, and progress efficiently.
Reborn Guide
1. Reach the required level for reborn.
2. Prepare your reborn card or required materials.
3. Talk to the correct NPC or use the reborn system.
4. Reborn rewards may include extra stats and progression bonuses.
Leveling Maps
Suggested leveling path:
- Early Game: Starter zones
- Mid Game: Death Prison / Fairytale
- Late Game: Bossran custom farming maps
