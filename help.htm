Reborn Guide
Reborn Guide

Max Level: 350
Max Reborn: 160

Reborn allows players to reset their level in exchange for powerful bonuses and long-term character progression.
Each reborn makes your character stronger and unlocks additional advantages as you progress further.

Requirements
• Reach Level 230.
• Prepare the required Reborn Card.
• Make sure your inventory has available space.

How to Reborn
1. Reach the required level for reborn.
2. Obtain and prepare your Reborn Card.
3. Right ClickRB Card in your inventory.
4. Confirm the reborn process to reset your level.

Reborn Rewards
• Additional character stats
• Increased power progression
• Access to stronger builds
• Long-term character development

Continue rebirthing your character to reach the maximum reborn level and unlock your full potential in BossRan.

Useful Commands
Stat Point Commands

For easier stat distribution, players can use the following commands in-game:

/pow 100
/dex 100
/int 100
/vit 100
/stm 100

Simply replace the number with the amount of stat points you want to add.
Gameplay Tips
- Hold Ctrl while buying or selling to skip confirmation.
- Press Alt while clicking items to preview them.
- AFK in allowed zones for contribution or rewards.
- Make a party before dungeon entry for faster repeated runs.
